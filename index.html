About Us

A&K Solutions is a web development studio based in India, built for one purpose — to give local businesses the digital presence they deserve, at a price that actually makes sense.

We work with gyms, salons, restaurants, clinics, and small business owners who know they need a website but don't want to deal with agencies that overcharge and underdeliver. We keep it simple: you tell us what you need, we build it fast, and we don't disappear after launch.
